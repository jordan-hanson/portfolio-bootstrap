My Book Discovered

Born in the city, but living in the country. I embarked on this journey through life in small town Central Valley. I grew up with my mom and grandparents, friends, and horses. Let's see and experience what has made me who I am today.
I have always enjoyed social adventure. My number one complaint in school with my parent teacher conferences was I was a, "social butterfly." I still remember when Mr. Johnson called me out for chatting with a boy named Frankie in third grade. I was so embarassed, but that characteristic still stands strong today.
My personality grew to be outgoing full of adventure. I grew up riding horses, traveled nationally to rodeo after rodeo, hiking, hunting, and even a little snowboarding at Brian Head, UT. My adventure has not ended. It continues to grow to what it is now and what it will become.
